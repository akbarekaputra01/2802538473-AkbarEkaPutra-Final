Neon District: The Future of Urban Combat

Prepare for the most innovative gameplay experience yet as we introduce Neon District, our newest map coming July 1st with Season 4.

Set in a futuristic megacity, Neon District redefines urban combat with its multi-level verticality and dynamic environmental systems that respond to player actions.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Lighting System: The entire map cycles through day and night modes, affecting visibility and strategy
  • Interactive Elements: Hackable security systems, destructible environments, and movable cover
  • Vertical Gameplay: Five distinct elevation levels with ziplines, elevators, and parkour routes
  • Live Events: Random events like blackouts or drone swarms that change match dynamics
  • Hidden Areas: Discover secret routes and loot caches throughout the district

The development team has spent over 9 months crafting this experience, with particular attention to competitive balance while maintaining the high-octane action our players love.

Neon District will be available in all game modes at launch, with a special 24/7 Neon Playlist running for the first two weeks of Season 4.

Stay tuned for our map walkthrough livestream on June 25th where we'll reveal more details and gameplay strategies.
