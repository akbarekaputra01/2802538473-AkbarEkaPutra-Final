PATCH • June 10, 2023

Game Balance Updates

This week's update brings major adjustments to the Phantom rifle and Sage's abilities, along with several other tweaks to improve competitive balance.

Weapon Changes

  • Phantom Rifle:
    • Reduced fire rate from 11 rounds/sec to 10 rounds/sec
    • Increased vertical recoil on first 5 bullets
    • Damage falloff now starts at 15m (was 20m)
  • Vandal: Slightly increased recoil recovery time
  • Operator: Increased equip time by 0.15 seconds

Agent Updates

  • Sage:
    • Barrier Orb health reduced to 600 (was 800)
    • Healing Orb now heals 60 HP over 5 seconds (was instant 60 HP)
    • Resurrection cast time increased by 0.5 seconds
  • Jett: Tailwind dash distance reduced by 10%
  • Raze: Boom Bot duration reduced to 5 seconds (was 6)

Other Changes

  • Added new sound cues for reloading and ability activations
  • Improved hit registration for high ping players
  • Fixed several map exploits on Bind and Haven

These changes are aimed at reducing the Phantom's dominance in mid-range engagements while maintaining its close-range effectiveness. The Sage adjustments should make her less of a must-pick in competitive play.

We'll be monitoring these changes closely and welcome your feedback on our official forums.
