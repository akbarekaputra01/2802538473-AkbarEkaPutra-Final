Fan Art Winners

May's best community creations featured. Winning designs become in-game sprays.

"Neon Dreams" by SarahK

Digital painting featuring Jett in a cyberpunk cityscape

We're thrilled to showcase May's top fan art submissions from our talented community. These three winning designs will be turned into exclusive in-game sprays available next month.

Each artist will receive:

  • Their spray featured in-game for 3 months
  • 10,000 Valorant Points
  • Exclusive "Community Artist" player title

Want to participate? Submit your artwork through our Community Hub by the last day of each month for a chance to be featured!
